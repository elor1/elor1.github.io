Beat Defender

Beat Defender is a game I made in about 2 months for the Grads in Games Rising star competition 2021.

It is a endless wave-based shoot 'em up game featuring procedurally generated maps and gameplay that is influenced by the game's soundtrack using audio analysis and beat detection. This analysis is used to adjust enemy stats and spawn times so that the game's difficulty increases as the music gets more intense.
